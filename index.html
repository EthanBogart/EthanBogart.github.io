Mr. Baseball Guy (GitHub)

Mr. Baseball Guy is a rather simple watchapp designed to nicely fetch and display live baseball scores as well as upcoming and previous matches.

The Pebble system includes more than one form factor, so a part of the challenge was figuring out how to display the information on three different watches: the Pebble Time, Pebble Time Round, and original Pebble. Another piece of the app is the notification center, which ran on a Raspberry Pi and would periodically send notifications to each watch on game start and end.

Developing Mr. Baseball Guy was a good experience in UI development, building an app from scratch, and customer service. Several kind users reported bugs and gave me a feel for what it means to have users firsthand.
